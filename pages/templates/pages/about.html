Paper Club how to:

  1. Create or join a club
  2. Propose papers to the club
  3. Score other proposals (top-scoring proposals are tracked and visible)
  4. Schedule a meeting and choose several proposals from among the top-scoring pool of papers to serve as candidates for the upcoming meeting
  5. Vote on candidates to select the group reading for the meeting
