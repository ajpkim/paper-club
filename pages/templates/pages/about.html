Paper Club how to:

  1. Create or join a club
  2. Contribute and score paper proposals within a club based on interest
  3. Plan a meeting and initiate paper election to detemine meeting reading
    • Planner selects candidate papers from among the club's highest scoring proposals
  4. Vote in election to detemine the paper for the upcoming meeting
  5. Read the winning paper
  6. Meet to share thoughts, questions, and ideas!
  7. Repeat steps 2-7
