Paper Club how to:

  1. Create or join a club
  2. Propose papers in a club
  3. Score proposed papers
  4. Plan a meeting and initiate election to detemine meeting reading
    • Candidate papers are selected from among the club's highest scoring proposals
  5. Vote in election to detemine the paper for the upcoming meeting
  6. Read the winning paper
  7. Meet and share thoughts, questions, and ideas!
  8. Repeat steps 2-8
